Light Studio Agreement

This agreement is for all dubbing, voiceover, or similar work done with Light Studio.
Please read the rules below and indicate your agreement.
Agreement Rules:
  • If you choose to work on a dubbing or voiceover session, you must stay until the end of the session. Do not leave in the middle unless you have written permission.
  • Arrive on time. Repeated lateness may lead to loss of future work.
  • Handle all studio equipment carefully and return it in good condition.
  • No food, drinks, or smoking in the studio. No disruptive behavior.
  • Keep all scripts, recordings, and studio information confidential.
  • If you break any of these rules, Light Studio may stop your work, ask you to pay for damages, or take legal action if needed.
  • Mobile phones must be set to silent mode during all sessions.
  • Respect other artists, staff, and their time; bullying or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • All work created in the studio is the property of Light Studio unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Sharing or posting behind-the-scenes photos, videos, or audio recordings from the studio is prohibited without written permission.
  • Follow all safety instructions and emergency procedures as directed by studio staff.
  • Personal guests are not allowed in the studio without prior approval.
  • Report any technical issues or equipment malfunctions to studio staff immediately.
  • Do not install, modify, or remove any software or hardware on studio computers or equipment.
  • Payment for services will be provided according to the agreed schedule; any disputes must be raised promptly.
  • Light Studio reserves the right to update these rules at any time; you will be notified of any changes.
By submitting, you confirm you have read and understood the Light Studio rules.
